About me

I’ve worked as a Graphic Designer and Pre-Press Technician in the printing industry for over 25 years. I crossed over into web design and development in 2014. I'm currently a UI/UX Designer and Developer.


I now design responsive, mobile-first websites and software using wireframes and mockups using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, and other software. All of my development experience is hand-coding for front end development in HTML5, SASS, CSS, jQuery, Javascript, React.js and more. Working as a UI/UX professional, I have the opportunity to collaborate with various teams and clients to design the best user friendly experience with appealing graphics to keep the user engaged and informed.
