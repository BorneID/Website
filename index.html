About

KATHY BORNE

Graphic Designer

UI/UX Designer & Developer

Currently based in Tampa Bay, Florida, I have worked as a UI/UX Designer and Developer. I design responsive, mobile-first landing pages, websites and web apps using software such as Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Miro and more. Most of my development experience is custom, hand-coded, front-end development in HTML5, CSS3, SASS, JavaScript, jQuery, React.js, JSON and more. I use tools such as Visual Studio, Visual Studio Code, Azure DevOps, TFS, GitHub, Jira and more. I collaborate with teams consisting of company stakeholders, sales, customer service as well as front-end, back-end and database developers to design the best ADA compliant and user-friendly experiences.


Starting my career as a Graphic Designer for print, I designed many fun projects over the years for clients around the world. Expanding my creativity from logos, flyers, brochures and postcards to product labels, package design, giant wall mural signs, architectural signs, ATM wraps and vehicle wraps has been a thrilling adventure. To see my designs around the USA and in other countries many years later is a proud accomplishment. My newest passion is creating motion graphic videos, music lyric videos and promo videos for social media, and I can't wait to see what comes next.
