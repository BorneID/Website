About

KATHY BORNE

Senior Graphic Designer

and UX Designer

Currently based in Tampa Bay, Florida, I'm a Senior Graphic Designer and UX Designer. As a Graphic Designer, I design projects for a wide variety of products such as logos, flyers, brochures, package designs, digital wall murals, architectural signs, vehicle wraps, tradeshow events, social media ads and more. As a UX Designer, I design and hand code responsive, mobile-first landing pages, websites and web apps for small websites and large e-commerce platforms.


I use software such as Adobe Creative Cloud, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Figma, Miro, Visio and more. I continue to advance my career by creating motion graphic videos, music lyric videos and promo videos for social media. Collaborating with company stakeholders, salespeople, QA teams, front and back-end developers, I create exceptional visual designs and user-friendly experiences.
